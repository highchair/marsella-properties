The Marsella Properties Team

Marsella Properties executive management is led by Christopher Marsella, President. Day-to-day operations is led by Rhoda Brown, Director of Property Management, with extensive experience in the management of office, mixed-use, parking, and retail properties with a strength in financial reporting. The Director is responsible for overseeing the effective operations of all Marsella managed commercial properties.
