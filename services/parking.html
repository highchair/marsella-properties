Our Parking Operations team is one of the largest in Rhode Island, providing facilities management services to thousands of parking spaces in Providence alone. With over thirty years experience in parking, we know how best to operate, leveraging technology with customer service, to ensure client and guest satisfaction. The parking experience is often times the first and last impression people have when coming to your property and we see it as part of a hospitality service.

Monthly Parking Portal

Visit the Parking Locator

  • Existing users may log in to pay their monthly parking or reserve different parking spaces
  • New users should use the locator to find parking. Enter “Providence RI” in the search bar
  • Once found, use the “Park Here” buttons to select that parking area
  • Proceed through the steps to set up an account and a payment option
  • The site is secure and accepts all major credit cards
