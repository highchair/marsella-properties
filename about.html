About Marsella Properties

Marsella Properties is Rhode Island’s premier property management and construction services firm, serving the urban core as well as suburban communities, from southern Rhode Island to Massachusetts. Our firm offers an in-house team of managers, property accountants, maintenance personnel and construction managers capable of addressing any and all issues related to property management and special projects coordination. We thrive at working in concert with a fully informed client, creating well maintained properties and fostering tenant satisfaction.

Management Philosophy

We recognize that no two properties are the same. As each building and/or tenant space serves a different purpose, it will be Marsella’s primary objective to work closely with owners to understand short- and long-term needs while removing the daily constraints of management. Our extensive services for all types of commercial assets provide local knowledge and resources that will maximize the potential of a growing portfolio.

Maintaining the property in excellent condition is paramount to the long-term viability of each property. Proper maintenance has a profound impact on the ability of current tenants to enjoy these premises as well as attract new tenants, ensure tenant retention, and overall client satisfaction. While working with each owner, we will find innovative ways to control costs while increasing property value and maintaining the highest levels of tenant satisfaction and comfort.
